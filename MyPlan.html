My Personal Plan 13 April - 12 May

Bangkok

📅 April 13 - 15, 2025
✈️ Arrival: April 13, 1:00 PM

Day 1 (April 13)

Arrival and Initial Exploration

  • 🚖 Check into hotel and rest until the afternoon

Day 2 (April 14)

Cultural Landmarks

  • 🏯 Visit the Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew

  • 🛍️ Visit Chatuchak Market (if on Saturday or Sunday)

  • 🌆 Visit Maeklong Railway Market
  • 🛍️ Tour the Talad Rot Fai Night Market

Ayutthaya

📅 April 16, 2025
Accommodation: One night near the historical park

One Day

  • 🚴‍♂️ Explore Ayutthaya Historical Park by bicycle
  • 🏯 Visit Wat Mahathat to see Buddha's head entwined in tree roots
  • 🏯 Visit Wat Chaiwatthanaram at sunset
  • 🚤 Boat tour around temples at sunset

🚌 Travel to Sukhothai by bus (6 hours) on the morning of April 17

Sukhothai

📅 April 17-18, 2025
Accommodation: Near the historical park

Day 5 (April 17)

  • 🚴‍♂️ Bicycle tour of Sukhothai Historical Park
  • 🏛️ Visit Wat Si Chum to see the giant Buddha
  • 🏯 Explore ancient temples such as Wat Mahathat

Day 6 (April 18)

  • 🚙 Tour nearby villages
  • 🍽️ Experience local food at Sukhothai Market

📅 April 19-21, 2025

Day 7 (April 19)

Day 8 (April 20)

  • 🚙 Trip to the Golden Triangle
  • 🍵 Visit tea plantations in Mae Salong

Day 9 (April 21)

  • 🛍️ Visit Chiang Rai Night Bazaar
  • 🏯 Tour Wat Huay Pla Kang
